To Perform Different Functions Of the Calculator:-
Click on the required option you want
To perform the basic calculations
In the drop-down choose which operation you want to perform.
enter the 2 numbers in the text-inputs and click on calculate.
To perform Conversion
In the 1st Dropdown Below choose which method conversion you want.
In the text-input enter the value you want to convert and in the dropdown next to it choose in which unit it is in.
In the 3rd dropdown choose in which unit you want the value in.
To Convert the value of different currencies
In the 1stDrop-down choose which currency value you want to convert.
In the 2ndDrop-down choose which currency value you want to convert.
enter the value you want to convert in the text-input and click on calculate.
